About the Creator

Reid Allen is a second year student at Algonquin College in the Graphic Design program. For his final assignment in his web design class he's making a 3 page website. After thinking about it for a while he realized that the music he likes to listen to is on multiple streaming medias and he wanted to change that. This is his solution, Music Hub, a place where all your favourite artist and songs are in one spot. Though he just started to code last semester, with the help from his peers and prof he is excited to present to you his website, Hopefully it helps you to find all your favourtie music.

About the Website

Don't you hate how you can only get some songs from certain media players? Well this website is your solution. Music Hub is a website where you can find all songs and artist from different media players, whether it's YouTube, Apple Music, Spotify, and much more. Browse songs and artists, then no matter where they are from you can download them to your prefered media player.
